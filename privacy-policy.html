Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Information We Collect

Precision Painting Co. collects personal information you voluntarily provide when requesting estimates, booking services, or contacting us. This includes name, email address, phone number, and property address.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide painting estimates and services
  • Schedule and confirm appointments
  • Send project updates and invoices
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our services and website

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services (e.g., payment processing) or as required by law.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and to improve your browsing experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at hello@precisionpaintco.com or (503) 555-0182.
