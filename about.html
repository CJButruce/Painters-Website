Founded 2008

Built on craft & reputation

Marcus Donovan started Precision Painting Co. in his garage with a used sprayer and a commitment to doing things the right way — even when no one was watching. Sixteen years later, that commitment is the backbone of everything we do.

We've grown from a one-man operation to a crew of 14 certified painters, serving over 2,000 homes and businesses across Portland and the surrounding metro area. Every job we take on gets the same attention and respect — from a single accent wall to a full commercial repaint.

  • Licensed Oregon contractor #CCB-182744
  • Fully bonded and liability insured ($2M coverage)
  • EPA RRP Lead-Safe certified
  • Sherwin-Williams ProPartner and Benjamin Moore affiliate
  • Oregon OSHA compliant with annual safety training
