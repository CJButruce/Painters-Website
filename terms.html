Our service agreement & policies

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Services

Precision Painting Co. provides professional painting and surface preparation services as described in written estimates. All work is performed by licensed, insured professionals in accordance with Oregon state contractor regulations.

Estimates and Pricing

Written estimates are valid for 30 days from the date of issue. Final pricing may vary from estimates if project scope changes, unforeseen conditions are discovered, or the client requests additional work. Any changes to scope will be communicated and approved before additional charges are incurred.

Payment Terms

  • Projects under $3,000: Full payment due upon project completion
  • Projects $3,000–$10,000: 10% deposit to schedule, 50% at project midpoint, balance on completion
  • Projects over $10,000: Custom payment schedule agreed in writing
  • Accepted payment: Check, ACH, Venmo, credit/debit card (3% processing fee)

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 72 hours before a scheduled project start incur no fee. Cancellations within 72 hours may be subject to a fee of up to $250 to cover scheduling costs.

Warranty

Precision Painting Co. provides a 2-year workmanship warranty on all completed projects as detailed in our warranty documentation. This warranty covers defects in workmanship and does not cover damage from external causes.

Liability Limitation

Our liability is limited to the contract value of the project. We carry $2M commercial general liability insurance and maintain worker's compensation for all employees.

Dispute Resolution

Any disputes shall first be addressed through good-faith discussion. If unresolved, disputes will be subject to binding arbitration in Multnomah County, Oregon under Oregon arbitration rules.
