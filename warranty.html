2-Year Workmanship Warranty

Every project completed by Precision Painting Co. is covered by our 2-year workmanship warranty — at no additional cost.

✓ What's Covered

  • Peeling, flaking, or bubbling paint due to application error
  • Paint adhesion failure on properly prepared surfaces
  • Bleed-through of stains we primed before painting
  • Color inconsistency or lap marks
  • Brush or roller marks visible from normal viewing distance

✗ What's Not Covered

  • ✕Damage from water intrusion after project completion
  • ✕Paint failure on surfaces that were incorrectly described or hidden
  • ✕Normal wear and fading from sun exposure
  • ✕Damage from impact, abuse, or negligent maintenance
  • ✕Surfaces that were not painted by our crew
ℹ️
How to make a warranty claim:

Call (503) 555-0182 or email us. We'll schedule an inspection within 5 business days and complete any warranted repairs at no charge.
